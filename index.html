👋 Welcome to your ADHD Budget Tracker!

Here's how to get started in 5 easy steps:

1
Set your currency & income Pick your currency at the top, then enter your take-home pay in the Income section. Choose how often you get paid.
2
Add your fixed bills Rent, phone, subscriptions — add each one with its due date. The tracker will colour-code what's coming up soon.
3
Set spending budgets In Variable Expenses, set a budget for food, transport etc. As you spend, update the "Spent" field — progress bars show how you're doing.
4
Log impulse purchases & check the summary Use the ⚡ Impulse Log to quickly capture unplanned spending. The Budget Summary at the bottom shows your full picture instantly.
5
Use the extra tools to stay on track Set savings goals, track debts, mark medication taken, build habits, and try the No-Spend Challenge. Everything saves automatically! 🔒
